Donuts

Description

Making donuts at home can be both fun and rewarding. Below, you'll find a detailed ingredient list and instructions, but here's a quick rundown of the basics:

  • Yeast: Active dry yeast helps the dough rise and gives the donuts a light, fluffy texture.
  • Milk: Warm milk is used to activate the yeast and moisten the dough.
  • Sugar: Granulated sugar sweetens the donuts and feeds the yeast.
  • Eggs: Eggs add richness and structure to the dough.
  • Butter: Softened butter adds flavor and moisture to the dough.
  • Flour: All-purpose flour is used to form the dough.
  • Vanilla: Vanilla extract enhances the flavor of the donuts.
  • Oil: Use vegetable oil for frying the donuts to golden perfection.
  • Glaze: Powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla are mixed together for a classic donut glaze.
  1. Activate the yeast in warm milk with sugar.
  2. Mix the dough ingredients and knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into donut shapes.
  5. Fry the donuts in hot oil until golden brown.
  6. Glaze the donuts and let them set before serving.
